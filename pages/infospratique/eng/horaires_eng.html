Period to travel to Cambodia

In Cambodia, there are two seasons : the rainy season and the dry season. The rainy season starts in May and ends in October. This time of year is not recommended to visit Cambodia. Indeed, during the period, the rains are abundant and accompanied with wind and the temperature is around 35 °C

The dry season starts in November and ends in April. The temperature is cooler during this period and the rains are much more rare.

The period from November to March is therefore the optimal period to go to Cambodia.
